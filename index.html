Who Is Sturgill Simpson?

Sturgill Simpson was born in Jackson, Kentucky on June 8, 1978. He's released seven full-length albums, two of which are bluegrass versions of older songs. He's most popular in the alternative-country and/or country-rock genres, however he is also well known for his distaste for overly commercialized country music.
